\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages automatic life consumption and food management for all players. 

According to the PDF specification\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Players consume 1 life unit per time unit automatically
\item Players start with 10 food units = 1260 life units
\item Players die when life reaches 0 and they have no food
\item \doxylink{classclientapi_1_1Food}{Food} is automatically consumed when life gets low
\end{DoxyItemize}

This class integrates with the \doxylink{classclientapi_1_1TimeManager}{Time\+Manager} to process life consumption every game tick and handles player death notifications.
